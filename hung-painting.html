How to hung a painting on a wall?

In this post I will show several ways to attach a baguette to your painting and to hung it up on a wall:

Using transparent cut outs from a plastic bootle

To implement this type of fixing you need to cut out four, six or eight (depending on the size of your canvas) cutouts from a plastic bottle. With a furniture stappler you need to stapple one part of the cutout to the canvas frame and the other to the baguette frame as shown in the photo.

Using specific hanging deviced bought from an artist shop

For this type of fixing you need to buy special nails sold at an artist shop. Sometimes they are not be easy to screw in. If this is the case, try to screw them in in a slightly different spot
