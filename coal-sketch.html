Why use coal to sketch for an oil painting

In this post I show you the advantages of using coal to draw the sketch on canvas:

Coal is the best solution for complex oil painting

If you want to paint a simple oil painting, the best medium for a sketch would be a well dissolved light oil paint, something like ocre.
Howewer, if you are planning a complex oil painting with lots of details and know that you might want to move items around your canvas, then there is nothing better than a coal stick.
